What is Kihap?

"Kihap is more than just a yell—it's the embodiment of spirit, focus, and power."

In Taekwondo, kihap (기합) represents the powerful shout that emanates from deep within during techniques. It's not merely about making noise—it's about channeling your energy, focus, and determination into every movement.

A proper kihap comes from the diaphragm, not the throat. It's the sound of confidence, the voice of your fighting spirit, and the audible manifestation of your mental and physical power. When you kihap, you're not just performing a technique—you're declaring your presence, your strength, and your commitment to excellence.

At Kihap, we believe every practitioner deserves to develop this confidence and power. Our AI-powered platform helps you perfect your techniques, build your strength, and develop the mental fortitude to unleash confident kihaps in every aspect of your training.
